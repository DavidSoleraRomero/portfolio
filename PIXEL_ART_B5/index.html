Introduction

Pixel art is a form of digital art that uses small, square pixels to create images. It originated alongside early computers and video games, characterized by its nostalgic charm and intricate designs.

History

This type of art emerged in the late 1970s and early 1980s, serving as the visual style for classic video games like Pac-Man or Super Mario. Despite technological advancements, pixel art has endured, evolving into a beloved artistic medium cherished for its simplicity.

Evolution

So, this lovely style of art has evolved a lot from its beginnings, so much that it has converted to a celebrated art form, with artists pushing its boundaries to create detailed compositions. Pixel art continues to thrive, offering endless opportunities for creativity and innovation in visual storytelling.

As pixel art continues to thrive, it presents boundless opportunities for artists to explore new techniques, push artistic limits, and captivate audiences with its unique charm and timeless appeal. Besides all of this, artificial intelligence is allowed to recreate images using this style. And it nails it!
